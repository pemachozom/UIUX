TasteHub: Online food Ordering

Problem Statement


With the daily change in the food menu, it becomes challenging for the customer to visit the restaurant personally and wait in a queue for the order. Therefore this project is to develop a application which allows customer to view the available menu daily and order food online.

Project Duration


Feburary 2024 to May 2024

The Problem


Pema Chozom is a customer who needs to order food from her favourite restaurant because she wants to eat delicious food

The Goal


Order food with specification and on time

What I learned:


Throughout this project, I learned the importance of user-centered design and how critical it is to gather and incorporate user feedback. Additionally, I improved my skills in wireframing and prototyping, ensuring that the final design effectively addressed user pain points and enhanced the overall user experience.
